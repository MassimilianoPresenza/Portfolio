More About Me

I’m a passionate marketing & communication specialist, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Communication & Multimedia Design at ZUYD University of Applied Sciences in Maastricht. With an MBO4 diploma in marketing & communication, I’ve built a strong foundation in understanding target audiences and developing innovative ideas — though I'm still refining my ability to express my creative visions through design.

I’m often described as someone with an “out-of-the-box” mindset, a creative thinker who loves to explore new concepts. My curiosity extends beyond the classroom, as I keep track of trends in gaming, tech, and digital art, from CS2 market dynamics to AI-generated art. I’m eager to improve my skills in 3D design and coding, alongside growing my understanding of UX/UI design.

In everything I do, I value attention to detail and collaboration. I believe that great work is built on strong teamwork, where you can build on each other’s strengths. When it comes to creative challenges, I always start with brainstorming, researching, and experimenting, often collaborating with my friends or classmates. My approach to any project is driven by a desire for continuous learning and improvement.

I’m a firm believer that with the right mindset, anything is possible. I’m hardworking, focused, and eager to dive into exciting projects that allow me to grow. Whether I’m helping to refine an existing brand, exploring digital art, or crafting intuitive UX/UI designs, I bring my full energy and enthusiasm to the table. And no matter the challenge, I’m always ready to learn from my experiences and improve along the way.
