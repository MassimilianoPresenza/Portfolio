Project:

Web Design - Daily Paper (Website still in progress)

For this project, I designed a digital prototype for The Design Papers, a well-known Dutch design magazine expanding its presence online. The goal was to translate the magazine's rich, creative identity into a functional and visually engaging website. I developed a homepage and a dynamic article page to showcase how content could be browsed, filtered by category (art, technology, design, ethics), and read online. The design also included a clear option for users to subscribe to the printed edition. Through this project, I gained experience in creating editorial-style layouts for digital platforms and learned how to balance aesthetics with usability in content-heavy environments.
